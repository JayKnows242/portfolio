01 — About

A self-driven developer who finishes what he starts.

I'm a full-stack software developer based in The Bahamas. Most of what I build starts with a real problem — a Bahamian business that needs to be found online, a community that needs a place to connect, a broadcaster who can't afford a $20,000 uplink — and ends with working, deployed software people can actually use.

My range is wide by design. I've shipped Django and React web platforms with PostgreSQL, real-time chat, and cloud storage; desktop AI assistants that listen, speak, and remember; a React Native mobile app; and a Linux-based bonded live-streaming system with its own adaptive networking logic and CI pipeline. I'm comfortable owning a project from architecture and database design through deployment and DevOps.

I'm especially motivated by technology that serves my country — tools that help Bahamian people, businesses, and institutions do more with less. I'm looking to bring that same energy to a team building technology for The Bahamas.
